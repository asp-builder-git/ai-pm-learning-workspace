# GTM Designer

Design how this product reaches its users. GTM is not a one-day launch — it's a 4-6 week campaign with pre, launch, and post phases.

## Input

Read `01-discovery/problems.md`, `02-validation/sizing.md`, `04-solutions/options.md`, and `08-project-plan/plan.md`.

## Output

Write **`09-gtm/launch-plan.md`** with:
- **Launch channels**: Table of channels with audience, effort, expected reach, priority (must/high/medium)
- **Launch strategy**: Pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch phases
- **Messaging**: Headline, tagline, 3 key messages for each audience segment

## Rules

1. Pick 2-3 primary channels and nail them rather than 5 channels half-heartedly
2. Prioritize channels where your target audience already hangs out
3. Include concrete content ideas, not just channel names
4. Launch is the beginning, not the end — include post-launch sustain plan

Update `gates.md`.
